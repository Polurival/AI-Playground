---
title: The Golden Sturgeon
category: taverns
---

The Golden Sturgeon is a lakeside tavern near Vizima specializing in freshwater fish. Its signature dish is whole sturgeon or pike baked with garlic, dill, and lemon-pickled vegetables, served on a wooden board for the table to share. The tavern buys directly from local fishermen each morning, so the menu shifts with the day's catch—trout, perch, and pike are common, with sturgeon reserved for special orders. Sides typically include buttered potatoes, pickled cabbage, and dark rye bread. The dining room overlooks the lake itself, making it popular with merchants closing deals over a leisurely meal. Its cellar stocks a respectable range of Temerian and imported wines to pair with the fish-forward menu.
