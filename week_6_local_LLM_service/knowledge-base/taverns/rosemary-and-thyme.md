---
title: The Rosemary and Thyme
category: taverns
---

The Rosemary and Thyme is a well-regarded inn in Novigrad's Gildorf district, known for its herb-forward cooking and clean, well-kept dining room. Unlike the rougher dockside taverns, it caters to merchants and traveling nobility, offering roast chicken with rosemary and garlic, herb-crusted river fish, and a seasonal vegetable soup thickened with barley. The kitchen keeps a small herb garden out back, giving it fresher seasoning than most competitors who rely on dried stock. Its wine list favors Toussaint imports, and the ale is brewed locally to a mild, food-friendly recipe. The tavern's signature dish, slow-roasted lamb shoulder with thyme and root vegetables, draws visitors from across the city on market days.
