---
title: The Chameleon Inn
category: taverns
---

The Chameleon Inn sits along a well-traveled trade road in Temeria, a modest roadside stop favored by merchants, mercenaries, and the occasional witcher. Its menu is simple and filling: bigos-style cabbage and meat stew, black pudding with fried onions, and coarse bread served with lard or butter. The innkeeper prides himself on never running out of stew regardless of how many travelers arrive, keeping a large pot simmering at all hours and replenishing it with whatever meat and vegetables are on hand. The ale here is cheap and strong, brewed in-house, and the common room is often loud with dice games and traveler gossip. It is not a place for refined dining, but reliable and warm after a long ride.
