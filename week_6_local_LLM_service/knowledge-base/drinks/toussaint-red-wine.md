---
title: Toussaint Red Wine
category: drinks
---

Toussaint red wine is considered the finest vintage in the known world, produced on sun-drenched terraced vineyards around Beauclair. Rich, full-bodied, and aged in oak, it is the drink of choice at noble banquets and diplomatic feasts across the Northern Kingdoms, often imported at great expense. Toussaint winemakers guard their techniques closely, blending grape varieties suited to the region's warm climate to produce wines with deep fruit character and long finishes. Beyond drinking, it is a key cooking ingredient in Toussaint cuisine, reduced into sauces for duck and lamb or used to poach fruit for dessert. A good vintage is judged by clarity, aroma, and how well it complements rich, buttery dishes—qualities that have made Toussaint red wine a status symbol as much as a beverage across the continent.
