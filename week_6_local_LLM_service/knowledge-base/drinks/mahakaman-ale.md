---
title: Mahakaman Ale
category: drinks
---

Mahakaman ale, brewed by the dwarves of Mahakam, is renowned throughout the Northern Kingdoms for its strength and rich, malty flavor. Unlike the thinner ales of human taverns, Mahakaman brews are dark, dense, and high in alcohol, reflecting dwarven brewing traditions passed down through generations of mountain clans. It is aged in oak barrels within Mahakam's mine tunnels, where a stable cool temperature deepens the flavor over months. Human taverns that manage to stock genuine Mahakaman ale advertise it prominently, as it is considered a mark of quality and often costs more than local brews. It pairs well with heavy, fatty foods—roasted meats, black pudding, and strong cheese—that stand up to its intensity. Dwarven drinking culture treats it as much a social ritual as a beverage, often accompanied by boisterous toasts and songs.
