---
title: Dwarven Spirit
category: drinks
---

Dwarven spirit is a potent distilled liquor from Mahakam, stronger and harsher than Mahakaman ale, often used as much for cleaning wounds as for drinking. Made from grain mash distilled multiple times, it carries a sharp, almost fuel-like bite that only seasoned drinkers—dwarves, witchers, and hardened mercenaries—handle comfortably. It is rarely sipped for pleasure; more often it is taken as a quick shot before a hard task, or used medicinally to disinfect a cut in the field. Taverns near Mahakam keep it behind the bar for regulars who know to ask for it by name rather than order it blind, as unsuspecting travelers who mistake it for ordinary vodka are often caught off guard by its strength.
