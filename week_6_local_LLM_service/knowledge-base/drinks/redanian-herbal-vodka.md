---
title: Redanian Herbal Vodka
category: drinks
---

Redanian herbal vodka is a strong, clear spirit distilled from grain and infused with local herbs—juniper, wormwood, and forest botanicals—giving it a bitter, aromatic edge distinct from plain grain spirits. It is drunk both socially and as a folk remedy, believed by common people to ward off colds and settle an unsettled stomach after a heavy meal. Redanian households often keep a bottle infusing herbs for weeks before it is ready, a tradition passed down within families. Taverns serve it in small measures, often alongside pickled vegetables or smoked meat as a palate-cleansing pairing. Its sharp, medicinal character makes it less approachable to outsiders than southern wines, but it remains a point of regional pride in Redania, where cold winters make a strong warming spirit a practical necessity.
