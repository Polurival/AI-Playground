---
title: Vizima
category: locations
---

Vizima, capital of Temeria, sits beside a broad lake ringed by farmland and orchards. Royal kitchens here favor game meats—venison, wild boar, and pheasant—paired with root vegetables and forest mushrooms gathered from the nearby woods. The city's markets are known for smoked meats, tart apple preserves, and strong dark rye bread. Temerian nobility prize rich sauces built on reduced wine and game stock, often finished with juniper berries or wild thyme. In the lakeside districts, fishermen supply pike and trout that end up baked whole with garlic and herbs. Vizima's taverns serve hearty stews to soldiers and travelers, while the castle's banquet hall is famous for elaborate roasts served on ceremonial occasions. The blend of forest game and lake fish makes Vizima cuisine a study in contrasts between rustic and refined.
