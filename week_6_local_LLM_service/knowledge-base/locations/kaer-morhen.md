---
title: Kaer Morhen
category: locations
---

Kaer Morhen, the mountain keep where witchers train and winter, is remote and self-sufficient by necessity. Supplies are limited, so meals are simple, calorie-dense, and built to last: salted meat, hard cheese, dried mushrooms, root vegetables stored in the cellars, and coarse bread baked in large batches. Witchers returning from long contracts favor thick, warming stews—bigos-style cabbage and meat dishes that keep well over days of reheating. Herbal teas and strong spirits are common, both for warmth in the mountain cold and because witcher alchemy occasionally repurposes kitchen herbs. There is little room for delicacy at Kaer Morhen; the cooking is functional, portioned for hard-working bodies, and rarely wastes anything. Preserved goods brought up from the valley before the passes close supplement whatever the keep's own small garden and livestock provide.
