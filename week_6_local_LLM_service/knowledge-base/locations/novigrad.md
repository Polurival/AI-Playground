---
title: Novigrad
category: locations
---

Novigrad is the largest free city in the Northern Kingdoms, a sprawling port on the Pontar delta ruled by its merchant guilds and the fanatical Church of the Eternal Fire. Its harbor brings in salted herring, smoked eel, and northern cod, while the crowded market squares of the Bits and Gildorf districts sell imported spices, dried fruit, and Redanian rye bread. Novigrad cooking blends working-class simplicity with guild-hall extravagance: thick fish stews and cabbage rolls in the poorer quarters, roast goose and honeyed pastries at merchant banquets. Because the city sits at a river crossroads, its kitchens freely mix Temerian, Redanian, and Kovirian ingredients, making Novigrad a natural birthplace for fusion recipes. The docks also supply cheap ale and vodka to the many taverns lining the canals, and street vendors hawk grilled sausages and pierogi to dockworkers and travelers alike.
