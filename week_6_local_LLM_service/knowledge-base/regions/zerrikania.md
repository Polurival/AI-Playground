---
title: Zerrikania
category: regions
---

Zerrikania is a distant eastern land known in the Northern Kingdoms mostly through traveling merchants, exotic goods, and its formidable warrior-women. Its cuisine, as it reaches the north, is characterized by bold spicing far beyond what Temeria or Redania use—chili, cumin, and other imported spices give Zerrikanian dishes a heat and complexity that stands out. Zerrikanian spiced eggs, a dish of eggs cooked with hot peppers and onion, have become a minor novelty in Novigrad's market stalls thanks to trade caravans. Zerrikanian ale, brewed stronger and spicier than northern brews, is prized by adventurous drinkers. Little is known of the full breadth of Zerrikanian cooking in the north, but its influence shows up as an exotic accent in border cities and trade ports.
