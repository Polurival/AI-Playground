---
title: Temeria
category: regions
---

Temeria is a fertile kingdom of forests, farmland, and lakes in the Northern Realms. Its cuisine leans on game meat—venison, boar, and rabbit—combined with root vegetables, cabbage, and forest mushrooms. Rye and wheat bread are staples, and dairy farming provides good cheese and butter for cooking. Temerian dishes tend toward hearty stews and roasts, often flavored with juniper, thyme, and bay leaf, reflecting the kingdom's woodland resources. Its lakes and rivers also supply pike, trout, and perch, which appear baked or smoked. Temerian cooking is considered a benchmark of "northern" Witcher-world cuisine: filling, rustic, and built around what the land and forest readily provide, without the refinement of southern Toussaint or the seafaring blandness of Skellige.
