---
title: Redania
category: regions
---

Redania is a powerful northern kingdom with a cooler climate and a cuisine built around preservation: pickling, smoking, and salting are common techniques given the shorter growing season. Redanian tables favor pork and goose, dense rye bread, pickled cabbage and cucumbers, and dried fruit compotes. Herbal vodka is a Redanian specialty, often infused with juniper, wormwood, or forest herbs, and is drunk both for pleasure and as a folk remedy against cold. Redanian goulash-style dishes—slow-cooked meat with paprika-forward spicing—reflect trade influence from the south. Overall, the cuisine is robust and warming, designed to sustain people through long winters, with less reliance on fresh produce than kingdoms further south like Temeria or Toussaint.
