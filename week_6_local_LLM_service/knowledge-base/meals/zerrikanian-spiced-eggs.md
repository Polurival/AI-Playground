---
title: Zerrikanian Spiced Eggs
category: meals
---

Zerrikanian spiced eggs are a novelty dish that reached Novigrad's markets through eastern trade caravans, prized for their bold heat compared to bland northern fare.

Ingredients: 6 eggs, 1 onion (diced), 2 hot peppers (chopped), 1 clove garlic (minced), 1 tsp cumin, pinch of chili powder, butter or oil, salt.

Method: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat and soften the onion, garlic, and peppers for a few minutes. Stir in cumin and chili powder and cook until fragrant. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and scramble gently, mixing them through the spiced onion base until just set. Season with salt and serve immediately with flatbread or rye bread to mop up the juices. The dish is meant to be eaten hot, straight from the pan, and is often sold by street vendors in small clay bowls.
