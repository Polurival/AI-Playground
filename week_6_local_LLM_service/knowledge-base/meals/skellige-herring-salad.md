---
title: Skellige Herring Salad
category: meals
---

Skellige herring salad is a staple coastal dish, built around the salted or pickled herring that sustains the islands through winter.

Ingredients: 4 pickled herring fillets (sliced), 2 boiled potatoes (diced), 1 small onion (thinly sliced), 1 apple (diced), 2 tbsp sour cream or yogurt, dill, salt, pepper.

Method: Combine the diced potatoes, onion, and apple in a bowl. Fold in the sliced herring gently to avoid breaking it apart, then stir through the sour cream until the salad is lightly coated. Season with salt, pepper, and a generous amount of fresh or dried dill. Chill briefly before serving if time allows, as the flavors settle well with a short rest. It is typically eaten with dense rye bread and is a common feast-day dish in Skellige longhouses, where preserved fish must be used creatively through the cold months.
