---
title: Toussaint Duck Breast with Plum Sauce
category: meals
---

Duck breast with plum sauce is a signature dish of Beauclair's refined kitchens, pairing rich poultry with the sweetness of Toussaint's abundant fruit.

Ingredients: 2 duck breasts (skin scored), salt, pepper, 4 plums (halved and pitted), 1/2 cup Toussaint red wine, 1 tbsp honey, knob of butter.

Method: Season the duck breasts and place skin-side down in a cold pan, then bring up to medium heat slowly to render the fat and crisp the skin, about 6–8 minutes. Flip and cook the flesh side briefly, then rest the meat. In the same pan, add the plums cut-side down and cook until lightly caramelized. Deglaze with the wine, add honey, and simmer until the sauce reduces and thickens slightly, then swirl in butter for gloss. Slice the duck breast and serve fanned over the plums with sauce spooned over the top. A staple of Beauclair banquets, it is typically paired with a glass of the same wine used in the sauce.
