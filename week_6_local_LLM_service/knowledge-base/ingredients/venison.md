---
title: Venison
category: ingredients
---

Venison, the meat of deer, is a staple game protein across the Northern Kingdoms, especially in the forested regions of Temeria and Kaedwen. Lean and slightly gamey, it pairs well with strong aromatics—juniper berries, bay leaf, thyme—and benefits from marinating or slow braising to tenderize the muscle. Roasted venison loin is a common noble dish, often served with a reduced wine or berry sauce to balance the meat's earthiness. Cheaper cuts go into stews with root vegetables and cabbage, stretched further with barley or dried mushrooms. Venison is typically hunted in autumn, so many recipes reflect seasonal pairings with apples, plums, and other harvest produce. It keeps well when smoked or salted, making it a practical choice for keeps like Kaer Morhen that must stock food through winter.
