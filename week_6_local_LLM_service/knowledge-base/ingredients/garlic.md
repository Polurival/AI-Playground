---
title: Garlic
category: ingredients
---

Garlic is one of the most widely used seasonings across the Northern Realms, cheap enough for common taverns yet versatile enough for noble kitchens. It forms the aromatic base for stews, sauces, and roasted meats, and is often paired with dill or thyme for fish dishes like baked pike or sturgeon. Garlic also has folk associations with warding off supernatural creatures, which has made it a staple ingredient travelers and witchers alike keep on hand—more out of habit and taste than genuine protection. It grows readily in kitchen gardens throughout Temeria, Redania, and Novigrad's surrounding farmland, and dried garlic braids are a common sight hanging in tavern kitchens. Roasted whole garlic cloves are a simple side dish in their own right, softened and spread on bread.
